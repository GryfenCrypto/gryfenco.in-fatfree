GryfenCoin Super Faucet

9 Jul

The super faucet draws its power from the one fourth of the mining transaction fees collected. Once a user has a minimum amount of coins staking in their wallet(threshold) then the super faucet will begin releasing coins to them.

This serves as an incentive to reach the threshold to generate continuous interest on top of the regular proof of stake mining at 0.2%-0.5% variable monthly rate. Once you have enough coins in your wallet you can slowly and steadily play Gryfen.co to earn even more GryfenCoins.

The amount of coins you must have to activate the super faucet is set at 50,000 GryfenCoins
