Gryfx.com Cloud Multipool

25 Jan

The Gryfen Multipool is able to use unused computer/hashing power of the users of the cryptocurrency options market Gryfx.com. Using a technology based on Node.js we are able to pool everyone’s hashes together and create our own pool.

We don’t use the hashing power from the GryfenMultipool in mining GryfenCoin. Instead we mine the most profitable altcoins automatically and trade them for Bitcoin.

We then take these coins on to different exchanges that have GryfenCoin listed and we purchase GryfenCoins off of an exchange with the Bitcoin we obtained by shorting the altcoins we mined.
