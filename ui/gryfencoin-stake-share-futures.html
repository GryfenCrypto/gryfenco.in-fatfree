GryfenCoin Stake Shares

9 Jul, 2014

GryfenCoin stake shares are for the future deliveries of the contents of the proportionate share percentage from the proof of stake coins that have been minted from the Premine that backs the house. Deliveries happen every week, and we accept Bitcoin, Coinbase, Dwolla and Bank deposit at Wells Fargo.

The Proof of stake coins from the house premine account do not mint at the normal 0.2-0.5% monthly effective rate. Instead the Gryfen Stake is 1.0-0.1% variable monthly interest - with no superblocks - with the intention to offer access to GryfenCoin to people who just wanted to buy it.

GryfenCoin Futures are sold up to 4 weeks out on Gryfen.co and can be traded after that GryfenCoin stake shares are an obligation for GryfenCrypto to deliver GryfenCoins to the share holder based on the weekly mint and the percentage of the shares.

GryfenCrypto is mandated to sell at least 95% of the coins staked every week but may keep a reserve not to exceed 5% of the staked coins. The Reserves are used to make investments in the GryfenCommunity with GryfenCoin for businesses or small projects that are willign to go bitcoin/gryfencoin first. The proceeds of the sales of GryfenCoin are fully transparent with open finance records and the proceeds only going toward development and marketing. Any unused proceeds that are not spent on Development or Marketing are then sent to GryfenVault after they have been exchanged for a stored value mechanism like mentioned above.
