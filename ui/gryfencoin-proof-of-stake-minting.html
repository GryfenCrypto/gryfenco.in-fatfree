Proof of Stake Minting

9 Jul

GryfenCoins have a weekly staking period and mint at a variable rate of 0.2%-0.5% monthly rate with weekly deliveries. So every 7 days you will receive some interest on your coins.

As a note, the GryfenCoin premine account of 250 Billion(that backs Gryfen.co) stakes monthly as well with simple proof of stake blocks that stake at 0.1-1.0% monthly interest and are presold as GryfenCrypto GryfenCoin Stake Shares(Cryptocurrency futures of delivery of GryfenCoin)

Proof of Stake blocks are appended to the end of the Proof of Work blockchain and are actually Proof of Stake convertible blocks - meaning in the eventuality of their destruction the coins will be destroyed like normally but then turn into new Proof of Work blocks at the end of the Proof of Work blockchain - effectively raising the coin proof of work limit of 100 Trillion - and causing a subtle form of inflationary pressure that would be very difficult to abuse.

With GryfenCoin when you stake your coins there is always a chance that you will get a Proof of Stake superblock. Proof of Stake superblocks are a special bonus to our users and an incentive for even the little guy to get involved because in our system anyone can get lucky.
