Gryfx.com Crypto Options

16 August, 2014

Users are able to earn GryfenCoins by participating in GryfX crypto-currency options market by trading, executing and speculating on crypto options that are backed by GryfenCoin. Gryfex is backed by the initial 2.5 Billion coin premine. In addition to that, one fourth of all mining transaction fees go back to the House account to make sure that it is always flush, but if full, any overflow will go directly the users.

There are three basic outcomes from purchasing an option. You can either execute on it and see a profit, execute on it and see a loss, or do nothing on an option you purchased and lose only the price of purchasing the option.

As a future feature, if the user consents, they can contribute their mining power to the Gryfen Multipool while using the options market. They can either help short bad altcoins or secure the future GryfenCoin Secure Transaction Network coming in the future. Microfees are collected through speculation on the market and they accumulate in a Microfees account that is open to the public. Microfees are sold online for bitcoins and then exchanged for rare earth metals/stored value items and added to the GryfenVault.
