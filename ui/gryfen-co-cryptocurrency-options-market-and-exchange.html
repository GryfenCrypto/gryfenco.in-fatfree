Gryfen.co Crypto Options

9 July, 2014

Users are able to earn GryfenCoins by trading on our cryptocurrency options market by trading and executing on crypto options that are backed by GryfenCoin. Gryfen.co is backed by the initial 250 Billion coin premine. In addition to that one fourth of all mining transaction fees go back to the House account to make sure that it is always flush.

There are three basic outcomes from purchasing an option. You can either execute on it and see a profit, execute on it and see a loss, or do nothing on an option you purchased and lose only the price of purchasing the option.

Coins that users lose on Gryfen.co go directly back into the House Account that backs the cryptocurrency exchange. If the GryfenCoin house account is full then those coins are burned and taken out of existence to be mined again. If they are Proof of Stake blocks they are then converted into Proof of Work blocks and then increase the Proof of Work limit gradually past 100 Trillion.

If the user consents they can contribute their mining power to the Gryfen Multipool while they are using the options market. Microfees are collected through the speculation on the market and they accumulate in a Microfees account that is public. Microfees are sold online for bitcoins and then exchanged for rare earth metals/stored value items and added to the GryfenVault.
