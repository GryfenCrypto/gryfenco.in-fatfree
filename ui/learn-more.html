Why an X13 Mod?

Our Algorithm uses the insanely popular X13 algorithm - extremely secure and energy efficient (compared to SHA-256 and others), but to differentiate ourselves from the horde of clone X13 PoW/PoS coins we add an extra twist to it.

Every block there are 4 algorithms chosen at random, with duplicity, for only 4 rounds of hashing compared to the traditional 13. Functionally this doesn't sacrifice much but it is more energy efficient and arguably just as effective at securing a blockchain. There's a possibility of 28,561 combinations of hashing.


Because we don’t want GPU Farms or ASICs getting involved and this helps to prevent both. We’re open to changing the algorithm as encryption advances inevitably.

Proof of Stake blocks will be added to end of the 100 Trillion Proof of Work limit- slow growth but enough to relieve against the constant upward price pressure. If these blocks are burned off they still exist but converted into future Proof of Work blocks that can be mined.

The Proof of Stake is higher for coins held in wallets locally, with Proof of Stake superblocks only occurring in the Wallet.
